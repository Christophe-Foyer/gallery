Picture of Greenland from the sky

Flew over Greenland yesterday, and managed to get this wonderful picture. Reminds me of how much I miss the Iceland.

The picture makes it look like we're lower than we are, and the window left a few smudges but oh well. Reminds me of how great summers in the arctic are though, so I thought I'd add a few pictures of my summer in Iceland 2 years ago.

Some of these pictures could use a bit less editing, I was a bit heavy-handed on the saturation (I still have the originals and might attempt to fix them up at some point, I tend to over-edit), but some of them really capture how beautiful Iceland can be.
